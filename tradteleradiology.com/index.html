About Us

Home

Welcome to T-RAD Tele Radiology Reporting Service, your premier destination for rapid and accurate medical imaging reports. At T-RAD, we understand the importance of timely and precise diagnoses, which is why we offer comprehensive tele-radiology reporting services for various medical scans including X-Rays, CT scans, MRI scans, PET-CT scans, hybrid scans, and USGs.

Founded by a team of experienced radiologists including Dr. Sithantha Seelan, Dr. R. Jenish, and Dr. Prem Anand, along with our dedicated team of specialized radiologists, we ensure that every scan is meticulously examined and reported with the highest level of expertise and care.

Rapid Reporting

Our cloud-based software, Medsynapse, enables seamless transfer and review of scans, facilitating swift reporting turnaround times. Whether it's a routine scan or an emergency case, our team is committed to providing prompt and efficient reporting, ensuring timely patient care.

Quality Assurance

At T-RAD, quality is paramount. Every scan is meticulously examined and reported by our team of radiologists, ensuring accuracy and precision in diagnoses. With our direct oversight, you can trust that each report meets the highest standards of excellence.

24/7 Availability

Medical emergencies don't adhere to regular business hours, which is why we offer round-the-clock reporting services, 365 days a year. Our team is always on standby to address your imaging needs, providing support whenever you need it most.

Nationwide Coverage
